**QuickBooks Setup Checklist**
**Phase 1 – Understand the Business**
1. Company Information
 - Company legal name
 - Entity type (LLC / Corporation / Partnership)
 - EIN
 - Fiscal year
 - Accounting method (Cash / Accrual)
 - Reporting currency
2. Business Operations
 - Main products/services
 - Revenue sources
 - Major expense categories
 - Business workflow
 - Industry-specific requirements
   
**Phase 2 – Gather Documents（收集资料）**
1. Banking
 - Bank account list
 - Bank statements
 - Credit card statements
2. Customers
 - Customer list
 - Opening Accounts Receivable
 - Outstanding invoices
3. Vendors
 - Vendor list
 - Opening Accounts Payable
 - Unpaid bills
4. Fixed Assets
 - Asset list
 - Purchase dates
 - Original cost
 - Accumulated depreciation
5. Loans
 - Loan agreements
 - Current balances
 - Interest rates
 - Payment schedules
6. Equity
 - Owner contributions
 - Retained earnings
 - Owner distributions
   
**Phase 3 – Design the Accounting System（设计会计系统）**
1. Chart of Accounts
 - Asset accounts
 - Liability accounts
 - Equity accounts
 - Revenue accounts
 - Expense accounts
2. Customers & Vendors
 - Create customers
 - Create vendors
3. Banking
 - Add bank accounts
 - Add credit cards
4. Tax Settings
 - Sales tax
 - Payroll tax
 - 1099 vendors
   
**Phase 4 – Opening Balance（录入期初余额）**
 - Bank balances
 - Accounts receivable
 - Accounts payable
 - Fixed assets
 - Loans
 - Equity balances

- Verify: Assets = Liabilities + Equity

**Phase 5 – Daily Operations Setup（日常业务）**
 - Customer invoices
 - Receive payments
 - Vendor bills
 - Pay bills
 - Expense transactions
 - Bank feeds
 - Rules (if needed)
   
**Phase 6 – Month-End Close（月结流程）**
 - Bank reconciliation
 - Review AR
 - Review AP
 - Record depreciation
 - Record accruals
 - Review General Ledger
 - Review Trial Balance
 - Review Financial Statements
   
**Phase 7 – Reports（报表）**
- Run:
 - Balance Sheet
 - Profit & Loss
 - Statement of Cash Flows
 - Trial Balance
 - General Ledger
 - AR Aging
 - AP Aging
 - Bank Reconciliation Report
